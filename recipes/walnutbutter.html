Walnut Butter

Home

Description

Walnut butter is a much healthier alternative to peanut butter, and by making your own you can save a lot of money.

Ingredients

  • Walnuts
  • Salt

Tools

  • Roasting tray
  • Food processor
  • Container

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Place walnuts in a single layer on the roasting tray
  3. Place the tray in the oven. The nuts should roast for about 8-10 minutes
  4. Remove the tray from the oven and let them cool for 5 minutes
  5. Put all of the nuts into a food processor and blen until smooth
