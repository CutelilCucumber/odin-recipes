Ghee

Home

Description

Ghee is a wonderful, healthy alternative to butter, only a bit more processing required! The shelf life is much longer and it has a tangy nutty flavor to go along with it's gut boosting effects.

Ingredients

  • Butter

Tools

  • Large pot (white bottom pots will help you see the browning)
  • Strainer or cheesecloth
  • Container

Steps

  1. Putt all your butter in a pot and boil it
  2. Let it simmer, stirring the bottom occasionally
  3. The butter becomes clear at some point, and it will begin to foam
  4. Stop stirring, only brush the top foam off to see the bottom of the pan. turn off the heat before the bottom blackens
  5. Strain your ghee using a strainer or cheesecloth into your container
